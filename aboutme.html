An Outdoor Enthusiast!

I'm an avid outdoor enthusiast who finds both joy and personal growth through cycling, mountain biking, hiking, and trail running. These pursuits have not only kept me physically active but have profoundly shaped my approach to challenges in all areas of life. Navigating difficult terrain on a mountain bike or pushing through the final miles of a challenging road ride has built my resilience and determination. Just as I've learned to adapt to changing conditions on the trail—whether it's unexpected weather, technical descents, or steep climbs—I bring this same adaptability and perseverance to my professional endeavors. My time in nature has instilled in me a deep appreciation for both setting ambitious goals and enjoying the journey along the way. This balanced perspective helps me stay focused and motivated while maintaining the flexibility needed to thrive in dynamic environments. AND, on top of all the wonderful energy, life and drive I get from being physically active, my next favorite thing to do after I have exerted all that energy is to dig into those STATS! All my data that has been analyzed and put into visualization charts, I love to see what I accomplished, how it compares to other similar activities, how I can get better, ride longer, or find other trails.
